Privacy Policy for Noniflow

Effective Date: April 21, 2026

Thank you for using Noniflow. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Noniflow handles information when you use the mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Noniflow is designed to respect user privacy. We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal information such as:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Messages
  • Photos or media files

2. Local Data Storage

Noniflow may store certain data locally on your device to provide core functionality, such as:

  • Focus timer settings
  • Break duration preferences
  • Task lists or notes created by the user
  • App customization preferences
  • Progress or productivity statistics

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers by Noniflow.

3. Permissions

Noniflow may request limited device permissions only when necessary for app features, such as:

  • Notifications (for timer alerts and reminders)
  • Vibration / haptics (for feedback interactions)

These permissions are used solely for app functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

Noniflow may use trusted third-party services in the future, such as analytics, crash reporting, or advertising platforms. If enabled, those services may collect limited technical information according to their own privacy policies.

Examples may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Crash reporting tools

If such services are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Data Security

We value your trust and aim to use commercially reasonable means to protect information stored within the app environment. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Noniflow does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If any such information is discovered, it will be removed promptly where applicable.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the official store listing or published support channels.

9. Consent

By using Noniflow, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
